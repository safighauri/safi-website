Safi Ghauri is a Pakistani barrister qualified at Lincoln's Inn. He is the Managing Partner of Esquare Legal and a Partner of Tahota Law Firm, one of China's top-100 global law firms. He is based in São Paulo.

His practice spans landmark litigation at Pakistan's Supreme Court, digital asset regulatory mandates across four continents, and multi-billion dollar cross-border transactions. In parallel, he has made over 300 broadcast appearances as a political analyst and spoken at Horasis, COP30, the European Commission's DARTE expert series, ETH LATAM and Stellar Meridian.
